Act 1

Act 1 starts off with hornet being transported in a cage surrounded by a strange binding that weakens her. after the binding is broken by a small butterfly-like creature, hornet falls down through the Pharloom into Moss grotto, the starting area. after escaping Moss Grotto Hornet colapses and is woken by the chapel maid, who informs you that the bugs that brought you to Pharloom were from the Citadel, after hearing this Hornet vows to find out why she was brought to Pharloom and thus the main quest begins. after exploring the second area, The Marrow, you will discover a path that has been blocked. finding you way up higher you will find access to a place called moss home, this is were you find the silkskill: silkspear, shortly arfter claiming the silkspear you will come accross a room filled with bells and a bug traped in silk at one end, after freeing it, it fights you jumping in and out of the bells and pulling them down from the roof, this is the bell beast. arfter defeating it, it disappears and a silk heart appears in the center of the room, arfter claiming the silk heart, which generates one spool of silk, you unlock the quick travel via the bell beast stations. defeating the bell beast opens the path to The Deep docks, the place you fight lace for the first time, exploring Deep docks leads you to a blocked of room leading to where Lace is, above the blocked doorway is a lever that opens the gate, although you cannot reach the lever without the silkskill: swiftstep, or dash. finding this leads you just out of deep docks and into a small part of Far fields, a large area overgrown with plant life. after unlocking the gate and fighting lace, she tells you she is "saving you" by killing you there and then, and not letting you face the world outside of Deep docks. moving on from lace you find yourself in the rest of Far fields you are forced to travel to one side of Far fields, being unable to ride up drafts of wind. you past by a large automaton called the fourth chorus, a large robot sent by the citadel to get rid of the pinstress who lives in Far Fields, one of two pinstess sisters who live throughout Pharloom, this one has retired and become the seamstress. after collecting the spines the seamstress asks for she weaves them into you cloak allowing you to ride the winds and glide through the air. when leaving the seamstress you are forced to fight The fourth chorus, after defeating them you can continue up through Far Fields until you reach greymoor, a large city like area home to the moorwing, the next boss on your journey to the citadel. Greymoor is where you first encouter Garmond and Zaza, they can assist you in the moorwing fight and in later fights in the game. arfter fighting and deafeating the moorwing you continue on to bellhart, a small village just beyond greymoor. After ariving in bellhart you find all of the citizens of bellhart suspended in the air by threads of silk coming from above the village. Hornet vows to find the source of the silk and free the people of bellhart. carrying on past bellhart leads you to Shellwood, a large forest like area with open large ponds and a variety of wild creatures that may or may not try to attack you. Continuing on through Shellwood will lead to fighting a large plant monster called Sister Splinter. After beating Sister Splinter you can carry on and find a path to the next silkskill: Cling grip, also known as wall jump. using this you can access the walls above bellhart, here you can find the source of the silk traping the residents of bellhart, Widow, a spider like creature resposible for weaving the silk threads snaring the town. defeating her grants you the silkskill: the needolin, a way of weaving silk around the needle and playing it like a harp, This allows Hornet to open weaver doors around pharloom. . Arfter defeating widow and freeing bellhart from the ensnaring silk that widow had spun, you can continue through Shellwood and up to The Blasted Steps, the last leg of you journey towards the citadel. Blasted steps is a medium sized area with windswept landscapes and bugs swiming through the sands. The harsh landscape is assisted by difficult platforming and enemies that deal double damage. After making you way through the Blasted steps you will come up upon the Grand Gate, The entrance to the citadel and the last thing blocking you journey. activating the lock at the gate produces a sound that resonates throughout pharloom and meets with the large bells that you would've come accross throughtout your pilgrimage. having rung all these bells the lock is released and the last judge awakens, the final test you have to pass to gain access to the citadel. having defeated the last judge the gate opens and you can enter the citadel ending Act 1 and the first part of the game.
